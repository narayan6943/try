  • Conduct A/B tests, user interviews, and usability studies to iterate on designs.
  • Support brand development and refine visual identity across channels.
  • Work within agile sprints to deliver fast and iterative improvements.
  • Think beyond aesthetics — design with conversions, engagement, and user intent in mind.
