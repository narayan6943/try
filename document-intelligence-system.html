What is Document Intelligence?

Document intelligence combines advanced artificial intelligence, machine learning, and optical character recognition (OCR) to automatically process, understand, and extract meaningful information from various document types. Unlike traditional OCR systems that simply convert text, document intelligence understands context, structure, and relationships within documents.

For logistics companies, this means automatically processing shipping documents, customs forms, invoices, and delivery confirmations with high accuracy, while identifying errors, validating information, and integrating data into existing systems seamlessly.
