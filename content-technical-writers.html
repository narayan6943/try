Content/Technical Writers

✍️ Content / Technical Writer
Location: Remote | Type: Full-Time
Job Description
  • Write blog posts, whitepapers, product documentation, and web copy.
  • Simplify complex AI/ML topics into digestible, engaging content.
  • Collaborate with engineers, product managers, and marketers to ensure accuracy.
  • Conduct industry research and competitor analysis to develop thought leadership.
  • Optimize content for SEO using proper keyword density, headings, meta tags, and internal linking.
  • Maintain a consistent brand voice across different formats and platforms.
  • Contribute to scripts for videos, onboarding guides, and FAQs.
  • Monitor content performance and suggest improvements based on analytics.
  • Publish content using CMS platforms (WordPress, Webflow, Notion, etc.).
  • Assist in newsletters, ad copy, and social campaigns when required.
