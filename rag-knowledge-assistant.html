What is RAG-Based Knowledge Management?

RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) combines the power of large language models with intelligent document retrieval to provide accurate, contextually relevant responses based on your organization's specific knowledge base. Unlike traditional chatbots that rely on pre-trained responses, RAG systems dynamically retrieve and synthesize information from your actual documents and databases.

For insurance companies, this means providing instant access to policy details, claims procedures, regulatory requirements, and product information with high accuracy and contextual understanding, while maintaining compliance and data security.
